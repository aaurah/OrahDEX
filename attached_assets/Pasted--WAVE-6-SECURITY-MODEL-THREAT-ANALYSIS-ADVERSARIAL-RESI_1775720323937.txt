Attack:

Mitigation:

Time‑locks ensure automatic refunds.
No funds can be stolen; only time is lost.

You must explicitly state this.

5. Relayer Security — Incorrect “Slashing” Claim

You wrote:

malicious relayers are automatically slashed

This is cryptographically impossible because relayers do not stake funds.

Required correction:

Relayers cannot be slashed because they never hold user funds.
Their only penalty is loss of future fees due to reputation scoring.

• sandwich attacks

8. AMM Manipulation — Missing Safeguards

Attack:
A whale manipulates AMM price to influence futures mark price.

Mitigation:

Mark price uses median of three sources; AMM alone cannot move it.
TWAP smooths short-term manipulation.
BSV oracle feed anchors external price.

Mitigation:

VAMM is simulation-only; no real liquidity is affected.
VAMM prices do not influence mark price or AMM pools.
VAMM cannot be used to manipulate futures or CopyVault.

This must be explicitly stated.

Mitigation:

Attack:

Mitigation:
