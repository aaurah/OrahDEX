✅ WAVE 2 — MATHEMATICAL CORRECTIONS & FORMAL CONSISTENCY

Below are all mathematical issues, inconsistencies, missing definitions, and formula corrections found in the white paper.

1. AMM (x·y = k) — Missing Fee Application in Invariant Update

Your formula:

Δy = (Δx × (1 − fee) × y) / (x + Δx × (1 − fee))

This is correct for output, but the paper never states how k updates.

Correct invariant update:

k_{new} = (x + \Delta x \cdot (1 - fee)) \cdot (y - \Delta y)


You must explicitly state:

The fee portion does not enter the pool; only (1 − fee) of Δx updates the invariant.

2. Price Impact Formula — Missing Absolute Value

You wrote:

impact = Δx / (x + Δx)

Correct formula:

impact = \left|\frac{\Delta x}{x + \Delta x}\right|


Because price impact is always positive.

4. Impermanent Loss Formula — Standard Form Missing

You wrote:

Correct standard form:

Your version gives a negative number; the standard expresses IL as a positive loss.

5. Concentrated Liquidity — Missing Liquidity Definition

You wrote:

Correct, but missing the definition of L:

L = \frac{\Delta x \cdot \sqrt{P_a P_b}}{P_b - P_a}


Or equivalently:

L = \frac{\Delta y}{\sqrt{P_b} - \sqrt{P_a}}


You must include this or the formulas are incomplete.
