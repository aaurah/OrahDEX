3. HTLC Script — Missing OP_DROP After OP_SHA256

Your HTLC script:

OP_IF
  OP_SHA256 <H> OP_EQUALVERIFY
  OP_DUP OP_HASH160 <TakerPubKeyHash> OP_EQUALVERIFY OP_CHECKSIG
OP_ELSE
  <LockTime_T1> OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY OP_DROP
  OP_DUP OP_HASH160 <MakerPubKeyHash> OP_EQUALVERIFY OP_CHECKSIG
OP_ENDIF


Issue:
Bitcoin Script requires <H> to be pushed onto the stack before OP_SHA256.
Your script is correct structurally, but missing OP_SIZE check for preimage length.

Fix (recommended):

OP_IF
  OP_SIZE 32 OP_EQUALVERIFY
  OP_SHA256 <H> OP_EQUALVERIFY
  OP_DUP OP_HASH160 <TakerPubKeyHash> OP_EQUALVERIFY OP_CHECKSIG
OP_ELSE
  <LockTime_T1> OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY OP_DROP
  OP_DUP OP_HASH160 <MakerPubKeyHash> OP_EQUALVERIFY OP_CHECKSIG
OP_ENDIF


This prevents malformed preimages.

5. AMM Impermanent Loss Formula — Missing Absolute Value

You wrote:

IL = 2√r/(1+r) − 1

Correct formula is:

IL = \frac{2\sqrt{r}}{1+r} - 1


But IL is always negative, so most papers express it as absolute loss:

IL = 1 - \frac{2\sqrt{r}}{1+r}


Fix: Use the standard form or explicitly state that IL is negative.
