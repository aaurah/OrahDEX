🜁 II. The Orah API — Full Endpoint Blueprint

Below is the complete REST API structure for your hybrid DEX.

---

1. Token Metadata

GET /v1/tokens
GET /v1/tokens/{chainId}
GET /v1/token/{address}


Returns:

• symbol
• decimals
• address
• chainId
• price
• liquidity
• Keeper‑specific fee multipliers


---

2. Price Quotes

GET /v1/quote


Params:

• chainId
• tokenIn
• tokenOut
• amount
• keeperAddress (optional)


Returns:

• expectedOut
• minOut
• priceImpact
• route breakdown
• Keeper fee discount


---

3. Allowance Target

GET /v1/allowance/target


Returns:

• contract address that needs approval
• recommended allowance amount


---

4. Swap Transaction Builder

POST /v1/swap/build


Body:

• chainId
• tokenIn
• tokenOut
• amount
• slippage
• keeperAddress


Returns:

• to
• data
• value
• gas estimate
• Keeper fee logic applied


This is the core of your DEX.

---

5. Transaction Simulation

POST /v1/swap/simulate


Returns:

• slippage
• MEV risk
• gas
• revert reason (if any)


---

6. Broadcast

POST /v1/tx/broadcast


Returns:

• txHash
• status
