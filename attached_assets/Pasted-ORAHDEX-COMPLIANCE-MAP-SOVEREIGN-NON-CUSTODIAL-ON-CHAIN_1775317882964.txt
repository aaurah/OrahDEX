ORAHDEX COMPLIANCE MAP (SOVEREIGN, NON‑CUSTODIAL, ON‑CHAIN)

🜂 1. Identity Layer — What OrahDex is

This is the foundation regulators look at.

OrahDex is:

• A non‑custodial, on‑chain trading protocol
• A Keeper‑driven execution network
• A smart‑contract AMM + HTLC router
• A self‑custody environment
• A protocol interface, not a financial intermediary
• A service provider, not a product issuer
• A tool, not a marketplace operator


OrahDex is NOT:

• A centralized exchange
• A custodian
• A broker
• A money transmitter
• A financial product issuer
• A token seller
• A data‑sharing platform


This identity is what protects you from heavy licensing.

🜁 2. Protocol Layer — What OrahDex does

Regulators classify based on functions, not marketing.

OrahDex functions:

• Users sign their own transactions
• Funds never touch OrahDex servers
• No private keys are generated or stored
• No admin keys controlling user funds
• No ability to freeze, reverse, or move assets
• All trades settle directly on-chain
• HTLC ensures atomic, non‑custodial cross‑chain swaps
• AMM pools are smart‑contract controlled
• No centralized orderbook
• No fiat ramps


This places OrahDex in the same category as Uniswap, Thorchain, 1inch.

🜃 4. Compliance Layer — What you MUST do

This is the only part that applies to you.

🇦🇺 AUSTRALIA (your region)

Australia has two layers:

✔ 1. AUSTRAC Registration (Mandatory)

This is NOT a license.
It is a simple AML/CTF registration required for any crypto service.

You must:

• Register as a Digital Currency Exchange (DCE)
• Maintain an AML/CTF program
• Keep basic compliance records


This is lightweight and does NOT require sharing user data with third parties.

✖ 2. AFSL License (NOT required)

You avoid AFSL because:

• You do not custody assets
• You do not issue financial products
• You do not run a centralized market
• You do not hold user funds
• You do not provide brokerage


OrahDex is a protocol, not a financial market.
