• Why the 25% bar turns red even when you press 100%
• Why your balance still shows after placing a limit order instead of going to 0


These two symptoms come from two different bugs, one UI‑side and one ledger‑side.

Let’s break them cleanly.

---

✅ 1. Why the 25% bar turns red even when you press 100%

This is a UI math bug, not a balance bug.

What’s happening:

Your amount is:

0.005841 ETH


Your available balance is:

0.0058 ETH


So when you press 100%, the form sets:

amount = availableBalance * 1.008 or 1.01 (rounding drift)


This makes the amount slightly larger than the real available balance.

Example:

available = 0.005800
100% button sets = 0.005841


That’s 0.7% more than your real balance.

Why the bar turns red:

Your UI logic does:

if (amount > availableBalance) highlight red


So even though you pressed 100%, the amount is not actually 100%, it’s 100% + rounding error.

The fix:

In the UI:

amount = availableBalance.toFixed(6)


NOT:

amount = availableBalance * 1.0 (float math)


This removes the floating‑point drift and the red bar disappears.

---

✅ 2. Why your balance still shows after placing a limit order (not locked)

This is the real bug — and the screenshot proves it.

What you expect:

After placing a SELL limit order for 0.005841 ETH, your available ETH should become:

0.000000 ETH


What you see:

Available: 0.0058 ETH


Why?

Because the UI is showing wallet balance, not DEX ledger balance.

Your backend does lock the ETH internally, but the frontend is still reading:

wallet.getBalance()  // raw on-chain balance


instead of:

spotFree[base]  // ledger free balance after locking


The correct behavior:

After placing a SELL order:

• spotLocked[ETH] = 0.005841
• spotFree[ETH] = 0.000000


But your UI is still showing:

spotFree = walletBalance = 0.0058


So the balance never changes, even though the order is locked.

---

🔧 The exact fix (frontend)

Replace:

Available = reportedBalanceFromWallet


With:

Available = spotFree[base]   // from /api/balances/spot


And compute:

Max Sell = spotFree[base]


NOT:

Max Sell = maxBuy   // this was your old bug


---

🔧 The exact fix (backend)

Expose proper buckets:

GET /api/balances/spot
{
  ETH: { free: 0.0000, locked: 0.005841 },
  USDT: { free: ..., locked: ... }
}


Then the frontend uses free, not wallet balance.
